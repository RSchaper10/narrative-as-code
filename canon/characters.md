# Characters

## Mara Vale

- Role: Municipal archivist
- Strength: patient pattern recognition
- Weakness: defaults to solitary problem-solving
- Story function: detects the gap between official record and buried system reality

## Ivo Mercer

- Role: Salvage diver and harbor contractor
- Strength: reads infrastructure through wear, noise, and risk
- Weakness: distrusts institutions so quickly that he can miss useful allies
- Story function: turns abstract clues into physical access and practical stakes

## Leda Sanz

- Role: Harbor operations director
- Strength: knows how to keep the town functioning during crises
- Weakness: treats secrecy as a form of stewardship
- Story function: bridge between public systems and concealed maintenance history
