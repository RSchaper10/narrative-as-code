# Coastal Siren History

## Why It Matters

The sample project relies on believable warning infrastructure, maintenance culture, and phased transitions from analog to digital systems.

## High-Value Facts To Verify In A Real Project

- How municipal or maritime siren systems are powered and maintained
- Typical failure modes in salt-heavy environments
- How often legacy systems remain active after partial modernization

## Scene Uses

- Tunnel equipment details
- Patchwork maintenance evidence
- Dialogue about why old hardware might still matter

## Warnings

- Do not treat every old siren as mysterious by default.
- Keep technical detail in service of tension, not display.
