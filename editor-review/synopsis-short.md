# Short Synopsis

After inheriting a surveyor's notebook, Mara Vale discovers that Signal Harbor's official maps omit an active tide station beneath the bluff. She teams with diver Ivo Mercer to investigate and finds a hidden chamber tied to the town's siren network. Evidence of recent maintenance suggests the system is still in use. The deeper mystery is whether the chamber preserves history, filters dangerous signals, or conceals a program the town still depends on.
